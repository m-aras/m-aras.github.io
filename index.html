About Project

Our project focuses on tourism. With the help of artificial intelligence, we aim to create personalized routes according to the places users want to visit and their interests. These routes are designed to include places to visit, accommodation options, and events on that date in that region according to the user's interest. For example, if the user is interested in concerts, we analyze concerts and festivals in the area between those dates and offer the most suitable route. The user can also narrow down their preferences with various filters such as budget.
